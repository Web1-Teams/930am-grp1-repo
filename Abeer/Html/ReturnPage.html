Electroniatak Policies

Payment Methods

The available payment methods are cash on delivery or via credit cards (Visa and MasterCard).

Customers have the right to inspect the product and ensure its condition before payment.

The payment method will appear on the order completion page.

Payment is made in NIS currency as indicated on the order completion page.

Exchange Policy

Ensure to keep the invoice of the received product attached to the package, as it is essential for a proper exchange process and for the information it contains, such as the package number and product code.

An exchange request must be submitted within 24 hours of receiving the package. This period is sufficient for the customer to check and verify the product received.

Exchange requests are submitted through live chat with customer service (visible at the bottom of the website and app). The customer must provide their phone number and the order number clearly stated on the attached invoice.

A symbolic exchange fee applies: 10 NIS for the West Bank, 20 NIS for Jerusalem, and 35 NIS for the 48 regions to cover part of the second delivery cost.

Exchanges are free of charge if a defect in the product is identified that was not apparent during receipt, provided the customer verifies the product with the delivery employee (e.g., size, etc.) to avoid exchanges later.

Products can be exchanged for others of the same price (excluding first delivery fees if applicable) or for higher-priced products by paying the price difference and exchange fees.

Exchange requests will not be accepted if the customer damages the product box or packaging, or if the product is used or incomplete. The product must be intact.

Exchanges are available for all products listed on the website except for perfumes, innerwear, and electronics such as phones and laptops.

Return Policy

Return requests must be submitted within 24 hours of receiving the package.

Return requests are submitted through live chat visible at the bottom of the website screen.

Return requests will not be accepted if the customer damages the product box or packaging.

The returned product must be complete, unused, and intact.

Return fees: 20 NIS for the West Bank and Jerusalem, 50 NIS for the 48 regions, if no defect is found in the product.

Return requests are processed within 5-7 official working days.

The delivery employee has the right to inspect the returned product before accepting it from the customer.

The delivery employee has the right to reject the return if the customer caused a defect in the product due to misuse.

The company reserves the right to reject return requests that violate the return policy.

Returns are available for all products listed on the website except for perfumes, innerwear, and electronics such as phones and laptops.

If payment was made via Visa or MasterCard, the refund will be issued to the customer’s card within 5 official working days.

Delivery Methods

Products are delivered through Mart’s dedicated delivery service.

Delivery is available to all cities and villages in the West Bank and Jerusalem.

Delivery time: 3-7 days.

Delivery fees for the West Bank: 20 NIS for orders below 250 NIS. Free delivery for orders of 250 NIS and above.

Delivery fees for Jerusalem: 30 NIS for orders below 500 NIS. Free delivery for orders of 500 NIS and above.

Delivery fees for the 48 regions: 65 NIS for orders below 1000 NIS. Free delivery for orders of 1000 NIS and above.

Delivery rules and fees apply to all products listed on the website.
